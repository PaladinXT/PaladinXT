COMMAND ABILITIES

RALLY: Return slain models

Pick a unit. Roll a die for each slain model. Return 1 model for each 6 rolled.

Note: Unit must be more than 3" away from enemy units.

START OF ANY HERO PHASE

AT THE DOUBLE: Auto run a unit

Auto run target unit up to 6".

YOUR MOVEMENT PHASE

REDEPLOY: Move a unit when an enemy unit moves close

When an enemy unit moves, runs, or retreats within 9" of your unit, you can move D6".

Note: Your unit must start and finish its move more than 3" away from enemy units.

ENEMY MOVEMENT PHASE

FORWARD TO VICTORY: Reroll charge roll

Reroll charge roll.

YOUR CHARGE PHASE

UNLEASH HELL: Shoot at charging unit

After enemy unit has charged within 6” of a friendly shooting unit, you can shoot them at minus 1 to hit rolls.

Note: Friendly shooting unit must be more than 3” away from all other enemy units.

ENEMY CHARGE PHASE

NO RETREAT, NO SURRENDER: Bonus attack

Pick a non-Hero, non-Monster unit. That unit cannot have charged this turn and is within 3" of an enemy unit. That unit cannot make pile-in moves in that phase, but add 1 to the Attacks characteristic of melee weapons used by that unit until the end of that phase (excluding those of its mounts, if any).

START OF ANY COMBAT PHASE

ALL-OUT ATTACK: Improve hit rolls

Pick unit. Add 1 to hit rolls.

ANY SHOOTING OR COMBAT PHASE

ALL-OUT DEFENSE: Improve save rolls

Add 1 to save rolls for targeted friendly unit.

ANY SHOOTING OR COMBAT PHASE

INSPIRING PRESENCE: Skip a battleshock test

Pick a unit to skip the battleshock test.

ANY BATTLESHOCK PHASE
