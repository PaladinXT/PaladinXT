REDEPLOY
Move a unit when an enemy unit moves close
ENEMY MOVEMENT PHASE

When an enemy unit moves, runs, or retreats within 9" of your unit, you can move D6".

Note: Your unit must start and finish its move more than 3" away from enemy units. Also you cannot shoot later in the turn.

ENEMY MOVEMENT PHASE

FORWARD TO VICTORY
Reroll charge roll

Reroll charge roll.

YOUR CHARGE PHASE

UNLEASH HELL
Shoot at charging unit

After enemy unit has charged within 6” of a friendly shooting unit, you can shoot them at minus 1 to hit rolls.

Note: Friendly shooting unit must be more than 3” away from all other enemy units.

ENEMY CHARGE PHASE

ALL-OUT DEFENCE
Improve save rolls

ANY SHOOTING OR COMBAT PHASE

NO RETREAT, NO SURRENDER
Bonus attack

Pick a non-Hero, non-Monster unit. That unit cannot have charged this turn and is within 3" of an enemy unit. That unit cannot make pile-in moves in that phase, but add 1 to the Attacks characteristic of melee weapons used by that unit until the end of that phase (excluding those of its mounts, if any).

START OF ANY COMBAT PHASE

INSPIRING PRESENCE
Skip a battleshock test

Pick a unit to skip the battleshock test.

ANY BATTLESHOCK PHASE

HEROIC ACTIONS

HEROIC LEADERSHIP
Get a CP on a Hero

Pick a friendly Hero and roll a die. On a 4+, it receives a command point.

Note: Add 2 to the roll if your general is slain.

HEROIC WILLPOWER
Non-wizard Hero can unbind/dispel

Pick a friendly non-wizard Hero. It can unbind 1 spell or dispel 1 endless spell.

THEIR FINEST HOUR
Improve wound and save rolls on a Hero

Pick a friendly Hero. Add 1 to wound rolls and 1 to save rolls for that hero.

Note: Cannot be used more than once in a battle for a given hero.

HEROIC RECOVERY
Heal a Hero

Pick a friendly Hero more than 3" away from enemy units. Roll 2D6. If the roll is less than or equal to that Hero's bravery, you can heal up to D3 wounds.

STRIKE AT THE OPENING
Fight in the hero phase (GC only)

Pick a GC within 3" of an enemy. That Hero can fight in the hero phase.

Note: It can fight again in combat phase, but it has Strike-Last.

GC = Galletian Champion

LEAD BY EXAMPLE
Fight in the hero phase (SB only)

Pick 1 friendly GC that has a SB and that has just carried out 'Strike at the Opening' heroic action. If that SB is wholly within 6" of that GC and within 3" of an enemy, that SB can fight in the hero phase.

Note: It can fight again in combat phase, but it has Strike-Last.

GC = Galletian Champion; SB = Sworn Bodyguard

SPELLS

ARCANE BOLT
Damage spell

Casting Value: 5

Range: 12"

At the start of any phase before your next hero phase, pick an enemy unit within range and visible to the caster. That unit suffers 1 mortal wound. If that unit is within 3" of the caster, it suffers D3 mortal wounds instead.

MYSTIC SHIELD
Improve save rolls

Casting Value: 5

Range: 12"

Pick a friendly unit wholly within range and visible to the caster. Add 1 to save rolls for attacks against that unit until your next hero phase.

GRINDING TEETH OF GALLET
Mortal wound bomb

Casting Value: 6

Range: 12"

Pick an objective within range of the caster. Roll a die for each unit within 6" of that objective. On a 4+ that unit suffers D6 mortal wounds.

Note: This affects friendly and enemy units alike.

MONSTROUS RAMPAGES

ROAR
Deny command abilities

Pick an enemy unit within 3" of this model and roll a die. On a 3+, that unit cannot issue or receive command abilities in the following combat phase.

STOMP
Deal mortal wounds

Pick a non-Monster enemy unit and roll a die. On a 2+, it suffers D3 mortal wounds.

TITANIC DUEL
Improve hit rolls when fighting a Monster

Pick an enemy Monster within 3”. Add 1 to hit rolls that target that enemy Monster until the end of the following combat phase.

SMASH TO RUBBLE
Deny terrain abilities

Pick 1 faction or defensible terrain within 3” and roll a die. On a 3+, defensible terrain is demolished (see 17.2.3) / warscroll scenery rules no longer apply to faction terrain.
